ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA FOR ANGANWADI WORKER

1. Anganwadi worker should be daughter-in-law of that village/ward and acceptable in the local community.
2. Female candidate of age 18 to 45 years
3. Candidates who have completed 10th or its equivalent qualification from a recognized Board/University are eligible to apply for ICDS Anganwadi Worker.


ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA FOR ANGANWADI HELPER

1. Anganwadi helper should be daughter-in-law of that village/ward and acceptable in the local community.
2. Female candidate of age 18 to 45 years
3. Candidates who have completed 7th or its equivalent qualification from a recognized Board/University are eligible to apply for ICDS Anganwadi.
